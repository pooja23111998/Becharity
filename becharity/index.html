Welcome to beCharity

Give new life to your old items by helping families in need!
We collect essential household items and distribute them to newly arrived families who have very little. That means that, among other missing items, they have no beds to sleep on, no table to eat on, and no pots or pans to cook with. Your donations can make all the difference for a family’s comfort and sense of security. Please review our FAQ below for a list of what we do and do not accept.
